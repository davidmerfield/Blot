Discounts
=========

I do not plan to offer any discounts on Blot. I know it makes business sense for me to run sales and offer promotions.

However, discounts seem unfair to my existing customers who paid full price. I also think there's something slighty tawdry about price reductions. It smells a little desperate in my mind. With that in mind, I decided to never discount the price of Blot for any reason. The price on the website is the lowest price it will ever be.
