# History

**2013** &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Blot begins as an idea.

**2014** &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Blot [launches on Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=8183498) and receives its first customer.

**2017** &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Blot supports its first full-time maintainer.

**2018** &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Blot receives its first volunteer contributor to the source code.

**2020** &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Blot hires its first part-time contributor.
