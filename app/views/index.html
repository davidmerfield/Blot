Blot turns a folder into a website. Files become posts on your site. Drag-and-drop to publish.

There’s no interface, just files, so you can use your favorite tools. Write your blog with Microsoft Word or Vim. Publish your photos from Lightroom. Work with others using Google Docs or git.
