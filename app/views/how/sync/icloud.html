iCloud

You can connect your site's folder to Blot with iCloud, a file hosting service available on most platforms. You can switch your site seamlessly to another client at any point.

Can Blot access all the files in my iCloud?

No, Blot only has access to your site’s folder.

Bandwidth limits

iCloud’s traffic limits do not apply because Blot hosts the contents of your folder on its own server.

File size limit

Blot can sync individual files up to 100MB in size from iCloud. Files larger than this will not be synced.

Hidden files and folders

Blot will not sync files or folders from iCloud whose name begins with a period.

Multiple sites

You can create more than one site using iCloud.
