Pages

Files inside a sub-folder called Pages become pages instead of site posts. Links to each page appear on your site’s menu. Pages are not added to the list of posts on your homepage.

You can change the order of the pages on your menu and add external links on the Links page of your site's dashboard.

Hidden pages

You can hide a page from your site’s menu with the Menu metadata. 

Menu: no

This page will not appear on your site‘s menu.

Create a landing page

You can create a landing page with the Link metadata. 

Link: /

This page will replace your site‘s homepage. If you still want your readers to access the list of your posts, consider adding a link to ‘/page/1’ to your site’s menu.

External links

You can add external links to your menu on the Links page of your site's dashboard.
