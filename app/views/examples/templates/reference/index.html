About the template

The layout of the grid itself was taken from Are.na with a few tweaks to my taste. The global navigation is designed to get out of the way. It features prominent links to your pages and a list of the tags used on your reference.

You can navigate between the full posts page using the arrows on the left and right. This is heavily designed around images.
