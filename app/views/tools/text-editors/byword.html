Byword

Create a website with Byword

Byword is a minimalist writing app designed to make writing notes and long-form texts easier. With its Markdown support, Byword works well alongside Blot.

How to use Byword with Blot

  1. Download Byword: Get Byword from the official site, the Mac App Store, or the iOS App Store. Install and open the app.
  2. Create site on Blot: Sign up and set up your site. Decide how you want to sync your folder with Blot.
  3. Write a new post: Open Byword and start a new document. Use Markdown to structure your content with headings, lists, links, and images.
  4. Publish your post: Save your post directly into the folder synced with Blot. Once saved, Blot will automatically publish your post to your site.

Additional Tips

  • Use Markdown to maintain consistent formatting across all your posts. Byword’s Markdown preview makes it easy to check your work before saving.
  • Organize your Byword files by saving them in a structured folder hierarchy that matches your site.
  • Take advantage of Byword’s keyboard shortcuts for extra efficiency.
  • Byword supports advanced Markdown features like footnotes, tables, and cross-references, which Blot automatically converts to HTML for your site.
