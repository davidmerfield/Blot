Why does Blot cost money?

Your subscription meets the cost of hosting your site and providing support. More importantly, it aligns our incentives with yours, rather than investors or advertisers.

Can I host Blot on my own server?

Yes, Blot’s source code is dedicated to the public domain.

Can I pay annually instead of monthly?

Yes, after you have signed up for Blot you can switch your billing interval from once-per-month to once-per-year on the subscription page.

Subscription renewals

If you pay for Blot annually, we will email you one week before your subscription is renewed to give you time to cancel or update your payment details. If you forget to cancel, contact us and we'll refund your payment.

Canceling your subscription

You can cancel your subscription on the account page. You can choose to delete your account immediately, or at the end of your billing period.

Payment processing

Blot uses Stripe and PayPal to process subscription payments.

Exporting your site

If you decide to stop using Blot, you can export your site to the Wordpress format, which is useful for migrating a site to another platform.
