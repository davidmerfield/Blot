Why does Blot cost money?

So that Blot can operate in your interests rather than those of advertisers or investors. Your subscription pays for the servers which host your site. We do not make money in any other way. We do not sell your data or the data of your readers.

Can I pay annually instead of monthly?

Yes, once you have signed up for Blot you can switch your billing interval from once per month to once per year on the subscription page.

Subscription renewals

If you pay for Blot annually, we will email you one week before your subscription is renewed to give you time to cancel your subscription or update your billing information. If you forget to cancel in time, contact us and we'll issue a refund.

Canceling your subscription

You can cancel your subscription on the account page. You can choose to delete your account immediately, or wait until the end of your billing period.

Payment processing

Blot uses Stripe and PayPal to process subscription payments.

Exporting your site

If you decide to move away from Blot in future, you can also export your site to the Wordpress format, which is useful for migrating a site to another platform.
