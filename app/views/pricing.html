Why does Blot cost money?

So that Blot operates in your interests rather than those of advertisers or investors. Your subscription pays for the servers which host your site. We do not make money in any other way.

Can I pay annually instead of monthly?

Yes, after you have signed up for Blot you can switch your billing interval from once-per-month to once-per-year on the subscription page.

Subscription renewals

If you pay for Blot annually, we will email you one week before your subscription is renewed to give you time to cancel or update your billing information. If you forget to cancel, contact us and we'll refund your payment.

Canceling your subscription

You can cancel your subscription on the account page. You can choose to delete your account immediately, or at the end of your billing period.

Payment processing

Blot uses Stripe and PayPal to process subscription payments.

Exporting your site

If you decide to move away from Blot in future, you can also export your site to the Wordpress format, which is useful for migrating a site to another platform.
