How to use Blot

Blot converts files you put inside a folder into posts on your website. You can connect your folder to Blot with any of the following tools:
Dropbox
Google Drive
Git

All of these files become posts automatically when you place them in your blog's folder:

Text and Markdown
Images
Word Documents
Bookmarks
HTML

Templates What kind of websites can I make?
a Blog for a Magazine a Photographer a Portfolio a Reference Developer
guide a Reference
About

So you can use your favorite creative tools. Blog with your favorite text-editor. Run your photography portfolio from Adobe Lightroom. There's no publishing interface, just your own files and folders.
