Pricing

Blot costs $4 per month per blog. You can host as many blogs as you like. Each blog includes:

  • Hosting
  • Support
  • All of the templates

Why does Blot cost money?

The fee pays for the servers which host your blog. It also covers the cost of providing support. Most importantly, it means Blot operates in your best interests alone, without care for the needs of advertisers or investors.

Multiple blogs

You can manage multiple blogs from the same account. Each blog is independent: you could use Dropbox with one and Git with another. Your blogs can use the same Dropbox account, or different accounts.

Can I host Blot on my own server?

Yes, Blot’s source code is dedicated to the public domain.

Subscription renewals

I will email you one week before your subscription is renewed to give you time to cancel your subscription or update your billing information. If you forget to cancel in time, contact me and I’ll send you a refund.

Canceling your subscription

You can cancel your subscription on the account page. You can choose to delete your account immediately, or wait until the end of your billing period.

Can I pay annually instead of monthly?

Yes, once you have signed up for Blot you can switch your billing interval from once per month to once per year on the subscription page.

Payment processing

Blot uses Stripe to process subscription payments. This means your payment details do not touch Blot’s servers.
