Pages

Files inside a folder called Pages become pages instead of blog posts. Links to each page appear on your blog’s menu.

You can change the order of the pages on your menu and add external links on the Links page of your blog's dashboard.

Hidden pages

You can hide a page from your blog’s menu with the Menu metadata.

Menu: no

This page will not appear on your blog‘s menu.

Create a landing page

You can create a landing page with the Link metadata.

Link: /

This page will replace your blog‘s homepage. If you still want your readers to access the list of your blog posts, consider adding a link to ‘/page/1’ to your blog’s menu.

External links

You can add external links to your menu on the Links page of your blog's dashboard.
