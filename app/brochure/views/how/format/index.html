Format your posts

A collection of guides to formatting your posts and pages.

  • Markdown
    A lightweight markup language with plain text formatting syntax.
  • \(\TeX\)
    A math typesetting library with powerful formatting features.
  • Layout
    A custom Markdown extension which offers simple layout directives.
  • Embedding
    Automatically generate embedded widgets from Youtube urls and other links.
  • Citations
    Use external citations in blog posts.
  • Wikilinks
    For internal links between posts.
