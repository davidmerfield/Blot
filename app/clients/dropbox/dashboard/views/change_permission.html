If you store sensitive data in your Dropbox folder, please consider the consequences of a security breach.

Partial access to your Dropbox folder

Blot has permission to access all of your Dropbox folder. Consider switching to partial access to your Dropbox folder if you don't need to change the location of your blog’s folder or share it with other people who use Dropbox.

If you switch from full to partial access, Blot will move your blog's folder into Dropbox's Apps folder.
