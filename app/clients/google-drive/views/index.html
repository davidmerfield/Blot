Can I limit Blot's access to a single folder in my Google Drive?

Not directly, unfortunately. Google does not offer the API features required to limit Blot's access to a single folder. Consider the following workaround:

  1. Create a burner Google account
  2. Connect Blot to your burner Google account and set up your blog's folder in your burner Google Drive.
  3. Share your blog's folder from your burner to your main account's Google Drive.
