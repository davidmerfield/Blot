Blot is a blogging platform with no interface. It turns a folder into a blog automatically. The point of all this — the reason Blot exists — is so you can blog with your favorite tools.

Use your favorite text-editor to write and Blot does the rest. Text and Markdown files, Word Documents, images, bookmarks and HTML in your folder become blog posts.



You can synchronize your blog's folder with Dropbox or Git.

Blot comes with seven templates. They’re easy to customize, lightweight and designed to fit both small and large screens. You can also develop your own template from scratch. Here’s a handful of the websites hosted on Blot:
