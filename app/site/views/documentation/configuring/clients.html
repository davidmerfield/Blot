Can Blot access all the files in my Dropbox?

Blot only requests access to your blog’s folder in your Dropbox by default. However, you can choose to grant Blot special access to your entire Dropbox. You should only do this if you want to move your blog’s folder, or share it with other people who use Dropbox.
