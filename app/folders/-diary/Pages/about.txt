# About

![Les Anderson](_les.jpg)

This folder contains a lot of images taken by [Les Anderson](https://unsplash.com/@lesanderson) between 1950 and 2005. They were kindly scanned from 35mm slides and dedicated to the public domain by [Nathan Anderson](https://unsplash.com/@nathananderson). 

It's mixed in with *Extracts from Adam's Diary* by Mark Twain.
