She has littered the whole estate with execrable names and offensive signs: THIS WAY TO THE WHIRLPOOL. THIS WAY TO GOAT ISLAND. CAVE OF THE WINDS THIS WAY. She says this park would make a tidy summer resort, if there was any custom for it. Summer resort—another invention of hers—just words, without meaning. What is a summer resort? But it is best not to ask her, she has such a rage for explaining.
